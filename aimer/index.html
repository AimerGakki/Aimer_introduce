Aimer (エメ Eme, [eme]) is a Japanese pop singer and lyricist signed to SME Records and managed by FOURseam.

Here's a time line of Miss Aimer's singer career

  • 1986 - born in unkonw, Japen
  • 1991 - lost her voice from an unfortunate accident
  • 2011.05 - her musical career began in earnest,released the concept album Your favorite things
  • 2011.09 - she debuted for Defstar Records, with the song "Rokutousei no Yoru"
  • 2012.02 - she released her third single, "Yuki no Furumachi/Fuyu no Diamond"
  • 2012.12 - cover album: Bitter & Swee
  • 2013.05 - Aimer released the single "RE:I AM"
  • 2015 - FSN: UBW
  • 2016 - Aimer announced her special 4th studio album "daydream"
